About us: Empowering Change, Inspiring Growth!

OVAST is a research, training, and consulting company specializing in offering up-to-date practical solutions for organizational and social development challenges. OVAST strives towards innovating sustainable solutions and developing competences within the public, private, and non-profit sectors. We do this through research, innovation, training, information dissemination, and quality technical advice.

At OVAST, we recognize that change is the most constant element in today’s globalized and highly dynamic world. For individuals and institutions to perform better at solving individual, organizational, and societal challenges, OVAST believes that we must devise new, strategic, and creative ways of managing change.
